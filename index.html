Research

Before diving into the design, I conduct thorough research to understand the target audience's preferences and behaviors. This helps in determining the type of styling that will enhance user engagement without being overwhelming.

Problem Statement

A well-crafted personal portfolio is crucial for showcasing skills, projects, and professional experiences in a visually appealing and organized manner. For individuals with limited experience in HTML and CSS, building such a portfolio can be a daunting task, hindering their ability to effectively present their capabilities to potential employers or clients.

Lack of Skill

Insufficient understanding of HTML and CSS leads to difficulties in structuring web pages, styling content, and ensuring cross-browser compatibility.

Design and Usability Issues

Without a background in web design principles, it is challenging to create an intuitive and visually appealing user interface that enhances user experience.

Time Constraints

Learning and applying HTML/CSS from scratch is time-consuming, especially for those balancing other responsibilities or working on a tight deadline.

Resource Overload

The abundance of online resources and tutorials can be overwhelming, making it hard to identify reliable and beginner-friendly materials.
